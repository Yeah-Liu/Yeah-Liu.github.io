<h3>More About My Recent Research</h3>
<div class="section">
<p>I'm now focusing on the Natural Language Generation and I have spent a year to build a classical Chinese quatrain generator based on the RNN encoder-decoder framework.</p>
<p>Chinese classical poetry generation is a very challenging task in natural language processing. We propose a novel method to generate Chinese classical poetry for a given essay or article, which is a pioneering work that few people have done before. Our method accepts a modern essay and generates poetry sentences one by one to form a complete poem relevant to the given document. Then poem sentences are generated based on the writing intent using a modified recurrent neural network encoder-decoder framework with output restriction. Subjective evaluations demonstrate that our proposed approach outperforms the state-of-the-art poetry generation methods and the quality of generated poems is comparable to ancient poems written by human poets. Most importantly, the poems generated by our approach are more relevant to the given essay. </p>
</div>
